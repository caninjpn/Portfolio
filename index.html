I am a Designer

and the beginning of becoming stems back into university, when I applied for the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University (SFU). The program nutured my interested that after graduation I pursued a career in UX Design. The pursuit eventually led to work as a UX Designer in Rakuten Inc.

One of the hallmark product I released was Rakuten Wallet, launched in April 2019. It's a Cryptocurrency trading service within Rakuten.
We began with a 2-day workshop to align the project members of the business team, the creative team, and the development team on what product we are building for.
As a result of the workshop, we identified our development direction to focus. With everyone's consensus I created personas and customer journey maps to affirm with the stakeholders how users will experience the app.
After a few more meetings and polishing the CJM we stepped into developing the app. Working closely with members of the app development team and the UI Designer we worked on the app and completed the app on-time despite several modifications of the criteria along the way.
This is Rakuten Wallet, a cryptocurrency app where a user can do simple cryptocurrency exchange on a easy-to-use interface. The design is based on a physical wallet, where each card represents a cryptocurrency and is initially stacked in a way where you can peek at the status of your cryptocurrency balance.
There are more works I could present but that'll be for another time. Tell me more.
