I am a Designer

The beginning of being a designer stems back into university, when I applied for the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University (SFU). The program nutured my interested that after graduation I pursued a career in UX Design. The pursuit eventually led to work as a UX Designer in Rakuten Inc.

One of the hallmark product I released was Rakuten Wallet, launched in April 2019. It's a Cryptocurrency trading service within Rakuten.
We began with a working Proof-of-concept prototype in 3 months time in order for the project to be greenlit for commercialization.
After the PoC ended with an overwhelmingly positive feedback.
from various stakeholders we held a 2-day workshop to align the project members of the business team, the creative team, and the development team on what product we are building for.
In the midst of development, I noticed miscommunication had begun to slow development and did a quick investigation to realize that there was no set workflow/ verification check of the production. I immediately held an emergency meeting with all the stakeholders to identify the problem and set out a solution to prevent further errors amongst each other. Since then development continued without a hitch.
There are other stories but that'll be for another time. Tell me more.
