As a passionate designer, I have continued to pursue design not just as a career, but as a hobby. During my Academic years I grew with asking the questions and aim to solve the issues that people have in life, and that's what I have been doing since.

At home, I constantly look for ways to improve life around me, doing DIY projects to create furnitures and mechanics that could improve my life. At work, I always look for customer needs and do my best to align it with the business needs. When something comes up I try to look at ways I can fix it, if it accommodates with the people around me. I want to share it with the rest of the world.

- Kevin Wong
